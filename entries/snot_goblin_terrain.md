## Transcription

### Poles

- Brass Scorpion

### Airbrush mix (drops)

- 2× Pallid Bone
- 2× Strong Skin Shade
- 2× drops airbrush thinner — *diary reads "Airbrush Thinner" on one photo, "Flowy Thinner" on the other*
- 1× Smokey Ink

## Notes

- Pallid Bone — Army Painter Speedpaint
- Strong Skin Shade — Army Painter wash
- Smokey Ink — Vallejo Game Ink
- **Thinner naming:** De keeps three separate products — Airbrush Medium (often called "thinner" informally), Airbrush Thinner, and Airbrush Flow Aid. The terrain mix above isn't fussy about which; the **primer recipe** (not yet photographed) uses a mix of all three.
